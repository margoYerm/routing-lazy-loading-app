Services scope

AppCounterService has local variable counter=0. This service used 3 times into different components. And this service registered in module ( 2 ways: provider in module.ts, or @Injectable in service). When it changed in 1 place, its changing in this 3 components (Creating service, App Counter for demo on this page, and in Services scope component, which shows on this page)

LocalCounter has same code. But it registered in using components, added property providers into @Component. And this counter changing just in 1 place!
